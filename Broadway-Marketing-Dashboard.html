In-Store & Physical Channel Window · shelf cards · counter · A-frame
    Physical Channel Rules
    • 🖼️
      Posters are your in-store billboard.
      One message, one product, one reason to look twice. Mount near the relevant section or at the entrance for Tier 1 events. Design in Canva, print locally.
    • 🖥️
      The till screen is your most captive audience.
      The customer is standing still, waiting to pay. That's the moment for a loyalty sign-up prompt, an event teaser, or a product they just walked past. Update for every Tier 1 anchor. Design in Canva — landscape 16:9.
    • 🏷️
      Shelf cards earn the sale.
      Customer is standing in front of the bottle. 20 words on taste and occasion converts more than a price tag alone.
    • 🅰️
      A-frame is for same-day impulse.
      Update at open. Game at 7 PM? The A-frame at 5 PM should say something about it.
    • 🚫
      Don't activate everything for every event.
      Tier 1 anchors get the full set: poster + till + shelf + counter. Tier 2: till + shelf cards. Tier 3: nothing physical. Restraint is what makes the Tier 1 moments land.
    • 🎨
      Canva templates for everything.
      Same fonts, same palette as digital. The store and the Instagram should look like they know each other. Build a poster template and a till template — lock them and reuse.
    Format Guide
    Poster Tier 1 only · print 1 week ahead · A3 or A2 · Canva → local print
    Till screen Tier 1+2 · update day-of · 16:9 landscape · Canva → USB or direct
    Shelf cards Tier 1+2 · 2 day lead time · hand-written or printed
    Counter card Tier 1+2 · 1 week lead time · A5 tent card
    A-frame All tiers · same day · dry-erase or printed insert
