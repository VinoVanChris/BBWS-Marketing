    Channels & Opportunities — What to Consider NextAdvisory · not yet in plan

    High Impact · Act Soon

    SMS / Text Marketing
    Grand Crew members already opted in. A Tuesday afternoon text — "New Okanagan arrivals, 6 bottles, in today" — converts better than email for day-of traffic.
    Pinterest
    Highest purchase intent of any social platform for gifts, wine pairing, and entertaining. Your gift box and seasonal content would perform extremely well. Zero local liquor store competition.
    In-Store Events & Tastings
    A monthly producer tasting or Grand Crew exclusive evening builds loyalty that no digital channel can replicate. Also generates organic social content.

    Worth Exploring

    TikTok
    Spirits, cocktails, and "what should I drink" content dominates TikTok. Your Without the Pretension voice is exactly right for it. Younger Kitsilano demographic is very active there.
    Local Influencer Partnerships
    Vancouver food and lifestyle creators with 10–50k followers convert better for local retail than national accounts. One partnership per quarter, product-based, no cash needed.
    Vivino & Untappd
    Claim your store on Vivino (wine) and Untappd (beer). Customers actively look up stores on both platforms before visiting. Free listings, high intent audience.

    Longer Term

    PR & Local Media
    Scout Magazine, Vancouver Sun food section, Eat Magazine, and Georgia Straight all cover local food retail. A pitch around your 40-year story or a notable new producer arrival is publishable.
    Neighbourhood Partnerships
    Co-marketing with Kitsilano restaurants and cafes. A card in their menus, their recommendation in your store. Mutual audience with near-zero cost.

    Google Business Profile

    • 1Add "Beer Store" and "Spirits Store" as GBP categories alongside Liquor Store — you are being missed by beer and spirits searches
    • 2Post on GBP minimum twice per week — Google rewards recency and activity with local pack ranking
    • 3Add a Products section with photos and prices — GBP products appear in search results directly
    • 4Reply to every review within 24 hours — review response rate is a confirmed local ranking signal
    • 5Add same-day delivery and province-wide delivery explicitly to your GBP description

    Website & Local SEO

    • 6Create dedicated landing pages: "Wine Delivery Kitsilano", "Spirits Kitsilano", "Beer Store Vancouver" — each targets a distinct search cluster
    • 7Build consistent citations on Yelp, YP.ca, and Foursquare — NAP consistency (name, address, phone) is a foundational local signal
    • 8Add LocalBusiness schema markup to your Squarespace site — tells Google exactly what you are and where
    • 9Target "near me" variants in blog posts — "best wine near Kitsilano Beach", "liquor store near UBC"
