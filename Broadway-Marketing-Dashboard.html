    Channels & Opportunities — What to Consider NextAdvisory · not yet in plan

    High Impact · Act Soon

    SMS / Text Marketing
    Grand Crew members already opted in. A Tuesday afternoon text — "New Okanagan arrivals, 6 bottles, in today" — converts better than email for day-of traffic.
    Pinterest
    Highest purchase intent of any social platform for gifts, wine pairing, and entertaining. Our gift box and seasonal content would perform extremely well. Zero local liquor store competition.
    In-Store Events & Tastings
    A monthly producer tasting or Grand Crew exclusive evening builds loyalty that no digital channel can replicate. Also generates organic social content.

    Worth Exploring

    TikTok
    Spirits, cocktails, and "what should I drink" content dominates TikTok. Our Without the Pretension voice is exactly right for it. Younger Kitsilano demographic is very active there.
    Local Influencer Partnerships
    Vancouver food and lifestyle creators with 10–50k followers convert better for local retail than national accounts. One partnership per quarter, product-based, no cash needed.
    Vivino & Untappd
    Claim our store on Vivino (wine) and Untappd (beer). People look up stores on both platforms before visiting. Free listings, high intent audience.

    Longer Term

    PR & Local Media
    Scout Magazine, Vancouver Sun food section, Eat Magazine, and Georgia Straight all cover local food retail. A pitch around our 40-year story or a notable new producer arrival is publishable.
    Neighbourhood Partnerships
    Co-marketing with Kitsilano restaurants and cafes. A card in their menus, their recommendation in our shop. Mutual audience with near-zero cost.
